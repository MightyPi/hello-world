About

Hello! I'm Ben. I've come a long way to get to Toronto where I live now, roughly 3,317 miles, all the way from bonnie Edinburgh. Or, if you want to sound like the locals, Edinbraaaagh! I'm a full time Software Developer, with skills in .Net(C#), SQL and Front-End Web technologies. I'm really interested in making technology that's easier for people to access data and services in many forms. Besides that, you'll find me going to gigs (Queens of the Stone Age was a recent favourite!), traveling, bingeing on TV shows, gaming, running along the waterfront (or trying to!), and always looking out for the next best ramen place! Oh, and I have a feisty Scottish cat to keep me in line, too. She's pretty good at boxing and she’s probably drinking a whisky as we speak…
